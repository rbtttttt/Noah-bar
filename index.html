To Love and Be Loved

Love is a feeling of deep affection and care for another person. It can be expressed in many ways, including words, actions, and gestures. Love can be a powerful emotion that can bring people together, create lasting memories, and bring joy and happiness to lives. This is a drink created by Noah, a young man who has been passionate about love for many years. He created this drink to celebrate the beauty of love and to bring joy to people.
This drink was introduced to Noah's bar in the spring of 2022 as a valentines day special. It is a sweet and delightful drink that is perfect for anyone who loves love.

  • Crystal Head Vodka
  • Candy Syrup
  • Lime
  • Lychee
  • Egg White

Flavour

  • Sweet
  • Refreshing

Bait & Bite

The Mekong Delta in southern Vietnam is a vast maze of rivers, swamps and islands, home to floating markets, Khmer pagodas and villages surrounded by rice paddies. Boats are the main means of transportation, and tours of the region often start in nearby Ho Chi Minh City (formerly known as Saigon) or Can Tho, a bustling town in the heart of the delta.
This drink was made as a tribute to Noah's home town bordering the Mekong Delta. it is a declious and herbal drink best enjoyed with seafood.

  • Lime leaf infused Gin
  • Smokey Mezcal
  • Lime & Yuzu
  • Dill
  • Splash of tomato juice.

Flavour

  • Smokey
  • Citrus & Herbal
  • Umami

Drop of Tangyuan

Tangyuan are a traditional Chinese dessert made of glutinous rice shaped into balls that are served in a hot broth or syrup. They come in varying sizes, anything between a marble to a ping pong ball, and are sometimes stuffed with filling.
This drink uses the many flavours of the Tangyuan to create a mimic of the Chinese dessert.

  • Sesame infused Vodka
  • Coconut infused dark rum
  • Butterscotch
  • Ginger
  • Side of Tangyuan

Flavour

  • Savoury
  • Sweet & Fragrant
  • Sesame & Ginger

About Noah

Noah is a master mixologist born in Mekong Delta, Vietnam, He started off as a bar-back for a Japanese bar and started to follow the path of mixology learning through his masters. Noah began experimenting with flavours combining the the balanced flavours of Vietnamese and Chinese cuisine to create a perfect sip desired in a cocktail.

Noah's Cocktails

Noah releases his cocktails every season, creating a master cocktail that will be added to his menus every year. Noah continues to find different flavour combinations every year to create an amazing cocktail for every season of the year.
